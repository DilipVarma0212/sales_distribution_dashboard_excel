### Overview

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance by comparing target vs actual sales, tracking trends, and identifying top-performing customers, products, and regions.
 
---

### Business Problem

The business lacks clear visibility into how sales are distributed across time, customers, products, and regions, making it difficult to identify top-performing areas and optimize strategies. This leads to challenges in tracking performance against targets and making data-driven decisions to improve overall sales efficiency.

---

### Data Source

The primary dataset used for this analysis is the "sales-distribution.xlsx" file, containing detailed information about each employee in the company.

---

### Tools

- Excel Power Query – Data cleaning, transformation, and structuring
- Power Pivot – Data modeling and relationship building
- Pivot Tables (Dynamic Tables) – Aggregation and analysis
- Dashboard Design – Interactive visuals and KPI tracking

---

### Data Cleaning Process

1. Ensured data accuracy for analysis
2. Removed null values and duplicates
3. Fixed inconsistent and incorrect data
4. Applied filters and slicers for better user experience

---

### Business Questions

- Which month recorded the highest sales?
- Which week had peak sales performance?
- Which customer contributed the most revenue?
- Which products generated the highest sales?
- Which region contributed the highest percentage of sales?
- Which country led in overall sales?

---

 ### Results

- March recorded the highest sales of 98K.
- Week 5 showed peak sales activity.
- Customer 33 generated the highest revenue of 61K.
- Product 30 & Product 41 were top-performing of 58K each.
- Western Region contributed the highest share of sales.
- Russia led in country-wise sales, followed by the USA.

### Recommendations

- Focus marketing and inventory planning around March and July to maximize peak sales opportunities.
- Analyze Week 5 trends to replicate strategies in other weeks.
- Retain and upsell to top customers (e.g., Customer 33) through loyalty programs.
- Increase promotion and stock for high-performing products (Product 30 & 41).
- Strengthen distribution in the Western Region and replicate its strategy in weaker regions.
- Expand business and partnerships in Russia and USA to leverage high demand.

---

### Outcome

- Quickly identify top-performing areas
- Monitor sales trends over time
- Make data-driven decisions for growth and optimization

### How to Use

- Refresh data using Power Query to update the dashboard
- Use filters/slicers to view sales by month, week, region, or product
- Compare Target vs Actual Sales to track performance
